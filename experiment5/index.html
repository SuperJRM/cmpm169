Experiment 5 - 3D Graphics

Description

Click and drag to guide the mass of cubes around the screen!

Technical

Eighteen rows of rings, each containing a random number of cubes, constantly lerp their positions toward the dragging mouse. Alongside this, each ring has their cubes and their distances from the canter change using a modified sine function, with each ring rotating along the X-axis using a modified 1/x function. The rings are then rotated along the Z-axis depending on the mouse's position on the Y-axis. A directional light is used to illuminate the boxes' metallic textures.
